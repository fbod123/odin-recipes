Coffee

Description

A classic coffee is the perfect way to start your day or get a quick boost. Made with freshly brewed coffee, it has a rich and smooth flavor that’s both comforting and energizing. You can drink it black or add sugar, milk, or cream for a softer taste. For extra flavor, try a sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder. It’s a simple, satisfying drink that’s perfect for any time you need a pick-me-up!

Ingredients

  • 1–2 tablespoons ground coffee (adjust to your taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • Sugar (optional, to taste)
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Optional: Cinnamon, cocoa powder, or vanilla extract for extra flavor

Steps

  1. Heat 1 cup of water in a kettle until it reaches a boil. If you're using a French press or pour-over method, make sure to let the water cool slightly after boiling (about 30 seconds) before pouring over the coffee grounds.
  2. Measure out 1–2 tablespoons of ground coffee (depending on how strong you like your coffee). Adjust to your preference.
    • If using a coffee maker, simply add the grounds to the filter.
    • For a French press, add the coffee directly to the press.
    • If using a pour-over, place a filter in the dripper and add the coffee grounds.
  3. Brew the Coffee
    • Drip coffee maker: Start the brewing process by turning it on.
    • French press: Pour the hot water over the coffee grounds, stir gently, and place the lid on. Let it steep for about 4 minutes, then press the plunger slowly to separate the grounds from the coffee.
    • Pour-over: Slowly pour the hot water in a circular motion over the coffee grounds, allowing the water to filter through. Keep pouring until you have 1 cup of brewed coffee.
  4. Add sugar, milk, or cream to taste.
  5. For extra flavor, sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon, cocoa powder, or a drop of vanilla extract.
  6. Stir well and serve hot! Enjoy your perfect cup of classic coffee.
